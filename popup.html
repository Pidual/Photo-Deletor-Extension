About Photo Deletor

Developer Notes

This extension runs a full neural network locally in your browser - no cloud, no API calls, complete privacy!

Model size: ~260 MB

Model: Fine-tuned ResNet50

Tech Stack

ONNX Runtime Web ResNet50 Chrome Extension JavaScript

How It Works

The AI analyzes each photo and classifies it as "memorable" or "forgettable" based on visual features. Forgettable photos (blurry, duplicates, low quality) are moved to trash automatically.

Made with effort and passion

GitHub: @Pidual

Open a photo in Google Photos
This will automatically delete photos marked as forgettable. Photos move to trash and can be recovered within 30 days.
